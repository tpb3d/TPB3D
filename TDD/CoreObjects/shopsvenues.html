Synopsis

Description

Shops and Venues are buidings with purpose.

Objects

Keosk - a small moveable building that sell food and/or goods.
Counter - building derivative that sells food/items where people just walk up to a counter but can't enter.
Shop - building derivative that sells food/items where people can go inside.
Diner - larger sit down eating building derivative.
Booth - a stand where a person or animation exists for people to buy tickets, ATM or information.
Entrence - building with ticket counter and turnstyles or gates limiting entrance.
